Professional Services

Leveraging more than 20 years of practical experience with customers across all industries, Crimson Quality is uniquely qualified to help you get the most of your Windchill Risk and Reliability (Relex) investment. Offerings include software training, reliability prediction calculation services and WQS (Relex) implementation services. Select from our Quick Start services, or engage Crimson Quality to help you configure a custom reliability or quality solution.

CQS-601

Reliability Prediction (MTBF) Services (remote)

A Crimson Quality expert consultant will take your bill of materials (BOM) and operating conditions to complete a standards-based reliability prediction. MTBF services are available using either MIL-HDBK-217 or Telcordia. Deliverables include an analysis report detailing failure rate/MTBF results, graphical output and an explanation of the methodology and results. At your request, Crimson Quality will also provide a copy of the WQS (Relex) project file used to complete the analysis.
